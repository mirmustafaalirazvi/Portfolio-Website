About Me

I am Mir Mustafa Ali Razvi, a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) student in 4th semester at New Science Degree College, Osmania University, Telangana. I have a strong interest in technology and continuous learning. I enjoy building clean, responsive websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I have worked on 12+ academic and personal projects including COVID-19 data analysis, Google Play Store apps analysis, clinical trials EDA, Diwali sales analysis, e-commerce sales dashboards, HR analytics dashboards, and SQL portfolio projects. My goal is to build a successful career in the IT industry where I can apply my technical skills, continue learning new technologies, and contribute to organizational growth. I strongly believe in consistency, self-improvement, and learning through practice.

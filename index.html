About Me

I am Mir Mustafa Ali Razvi, a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) student in 4th semester at New Science Degree College, Osmania University, Telangana. I have a strong interest in technology and continuous learning. I enjoy building clean, responsive websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I have completed a one-month internship at Unified Mentor, where I gained practical exposure to real-world data analysis and business operations. Through this experience, I strengthened my analytical thinking and learned how data supports decision-making in organizations.

I have worked on 12+ academic and personal projects including COVID-19 data analysis, Google Play Store apps analysis, clinical trials EDA, Diwali sales analysis, e-commerce sales dashboards, HR analytics dashboards, and SQL portfolio projects. These projects helped me gain hands-on experience in Python, SQL, Power BI, and Excel.

I am skilled in SQL, Power BI,PowerPoint, and Excel and enjoy creating interactive dashboards that provide meaningful business insights. I have also worked on Java, C, and C++ projects, which helped me build strong programming fundamentals and problem-solving abilities.

My goal is to build a successful career in the IT industry where I can apply my technical skills, continue learning new technologies, and contribute to organizational growth. I strongly believe in consistency, self-improvement, and learning through practice.
